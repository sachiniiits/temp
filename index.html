Built the public platform for a not-for-profit

I engineered the public platform for Janartham Foundation, a Section 8 not-for-profit working on sustainable livelihoods, climate resilience, child rights and public health. The build was a decoupled full stack: a React 19 and Vite single page app paired with a Strapi v5 headless CMS over REST, backed by PostgreSQL in production with SQLite for local parity.

I shipped the CSR partnership, thematic programme and impact metrics pages with Framer Motion sequences, plus a CMS driven blog that lets non-technical staff publish without ever touching code.
